# Skip to main

The skip to main - also known as skip navigation links - is a way for the user to skip repeated content on a page, which usually is the navigation.
It is made to meet the [Succes Criteria 2.4.1 Bypass Blocks](https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG21/#bypass-blocks)
